Clients Question

Got questions? Check out the most frequently asked questions by our clients. Find answers to inquiries about memberships, events, and more

Q: How can I join the Junior Program?

A: Joining is easy! Visit our website's Junior Program section and follow the simple registration process.

Q: What age group does the Teenager Program cater to?

A: The Teenager Program is designed for individuals aged 13 to 18 who are passionate about honing their hockey skills.

Q: Are there any trial sessions available for new members?

A: Yes, we offer trial sessions for newcomers. Check our schedule for upcoming trial dates and join us on the field.

Q: Can I sponsor an event or team?

A: Absolutely! We welcome sponsorships. Contact our sponsorship team to discuss exciting opportunities and benefits.

Q: How do I inquire about job opportunities with your organization?

A: Explore our Careers page on the website. If you find a suitable position, follow the application instructions provided.

Q: What safety measures are in place during training sessions?

A: Safety is our top priority. We follow strict protocols, including certified coaches, first aid availability, and sanitized equipment.
